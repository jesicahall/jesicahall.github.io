    • Manners in the Media Center

    About

    In this video, you will learn the rules of the media center. Understanding the rules of the media center is important for each and every student to know in order that they can demonstrate the expected and proper behavior that everyone should be exercising. Watch the video above and read over the list of rules below.

    Rules:
    1. Use inside voice to not disturb others
    2. Always say "please" when asking for help
    3. Never eat or drink foods in the media center
    4. Be respectful of the items in the room
    5. Remember that you can only check-out two books at a time
    6. Always read silently to yourself
    7. Always return your books on time
    8. Remember to stay silent during reading time to respect others
    9. Do not interrupt the teacher when they are reading

    Media Center Checkout Rules

    About

    In this video, you will learn the checkout rules of the media center. Understanding the rules of the media center is important for each and every student to know in order that they can demonstrate the expected and proper behavior that everyone should be exercising. Watch the video above and read over the list of rules below.

    Rules:
    1. When entering the media center, sit in your assigned seat
    2. Sit quietly with your books in front of you
    3. Do not get out of your seat unless asked
    4. Make sure Destiny is on "Check in" when checking in a book
    5. Use the barcode on the back of the book to scan the book
    6. Make sure the barcode says "Hickory Flat Elementary" before scanning
    7. When returning a book, make sure to place the book edge facing outward on the shelf, using both hands
    8. After you check-in your book, place the book on the cart
    9. Use the dot-chart to find a book in your grade level
    10. When you are ready to check-out a book, go to the circulation desk

    Congratulations!

    Now you know the rules!!
