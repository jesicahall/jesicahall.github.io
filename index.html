Hickory Flat Media Center

Purpose

The media center at Hickory Flat Elementary houses over 20,000 books ready to be check-out and read by all of you! As you go through the website, you will find out about our rules of the media center, the check-ins and check-outs, the Dewey decimal system, and Destiny.
